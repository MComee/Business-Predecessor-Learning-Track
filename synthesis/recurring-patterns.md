# Recurring Patterns

This artifact tracks recurring patterns observed across multiple historical targets.

## Use

Add to this file only when a pattern appears across more than one target or when a single target reveals a pattern likely worth testing against future targets.

## Pattern Entry Template

```markdown
## Pattern: <Name>

### Summary

### Targets observed

### Evidence

### Four Pillars involved

- Business & Organizations:
- Leadership & Human Systems:
- Capital & Wealth:
- Decision Making & Judgment:

### Lessons to copy

### Lessons to avoid

### Open questions
```
