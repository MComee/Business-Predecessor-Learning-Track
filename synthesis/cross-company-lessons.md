# Cross-Company Lessons

This artifact tracks lessons that emerge from comparing multiple companies or organizations.

## Use

Add to this file when two or more company targets reveal a useful similarity, difference, contradiction, or reusable lesson.

## Lesson Entry Template

```markdown
## Lesson: <Name>

### Companies compared

### Summary

### What matched

### What differed

### Evidence by company

### Lesson to copy

### Lesson to avoid

### Limits of the lesson

### Open questions
```
