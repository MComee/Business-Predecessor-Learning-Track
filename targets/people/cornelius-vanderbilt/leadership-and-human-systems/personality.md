# Cornelius Vanderbilt — Personality

## Report Purpose

This report will examine Vanderbilt's personality, temperament, reputation, habits, and personal operating style as historical evidence permits.

## Evidence Status

Scaffold only. Personality claims must be source-grounded and separated from myth, reputation, and later interpretation.

## Core Questions

- What traits are consistently reported across credible sources?
- Which traits are supported by documented behavior?
- Which traits are reputation, legend, or later simplification?
- How did temperament affect business outcomes?
- Which traits are useful to study but dangerous to copy blindly?
