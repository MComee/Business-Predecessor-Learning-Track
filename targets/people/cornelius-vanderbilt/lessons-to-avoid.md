# Cornelius Vanderbilt — Lessons to Avoid

## Evidence Status

Scaffold only. No lesson should be treated as final until supported by sources.

## Candidate Risk Areas

- Do not confuse ruthless behavior with universally transferable business skill.
- Do not copy strategies that depended on weak regulation, coercive leverage, or ethically questionable conduct.
- Do not treat historical wealth accumulation as proof that all methods used were acceptable or reusable.
- Do not ignore succession, family control, reputation, or long-term institutional consequences.
